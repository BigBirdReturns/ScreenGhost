# Example Goals for Screen Ghost

## Easy (1-3 taps)
Open Settings
Open the camera app
Turn on the flashlight
Take a screenshot
Open Chrome

## Medium (3-7 taps)
Open Settings and turn on Dark Mode
Open Settings and turn on WiFi
Open Settings and enable Bluetooth
Open Chrome and search for weather
Open Calendar and check tomorrow's events

## Hard (7+ taps)
Open Google Home and set living room lights to 50%
Open Nest app and set thermostat to 72 degrees
Open Settings and change the wallpaper
Open Chrome, go to gmail.com, and compose a new email
Open Calendar and create an event for tomorrow at 3pm called "Meeting"

## Smart Home (requires apps installed)
Open SmartThings and turn off all lights
Open Philips Hue and set bedroom to warm white
Open Ecobee and set temperature to 70
Open Ring and check the front door camera
Open August and lock the front door

## Notes

- Start with easy goals to verify your setup works
- Medium goals are good for demos
- Hard goals may require --max-steps 15 or higher
- Smart home goals require the relevant apps installed and logged in
